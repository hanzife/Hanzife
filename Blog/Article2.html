What is a style guide?

the one thing that makes the team (designers, developers, marketing team & copywrites) read a design and speak the same languege is called the style guide.

a style guide is a document which contains sets of rules or standards for the graphic style, colour, typography, iconography, imagery and principles of a brand and gives direction on how to use it. And makes it easier for these stakeholders (the team) to implement the design and read directions.
